Phase 1: Initial Development

Budget Allocation: ₹25,000 Crore

1. Education and Healthcare Sector

  • Revenue Source: Government funding, private investments, and school fees
  • Schools: 75 schools, institutes connected to multinational corporations
  • Healthcare: 1 hospital and 7 clinics per sector
  • State Permissions: Land allotment for schools and hospitals, health department approval
  • Central Permissions: Educational certifications, health ministry clearances

2. Agriculture & Food Processing Sector

  • Revenue Source: Export of staple crops (Rice), food processing units
  • Budget Allocation: ₹74,300 Crore
  • Expected Revenue: Exports of processed foods, revenue from local agriculture markets
  • State Permissions: Irrigation project approvals, agricultural licensing
  • Central Permissions: Export permissions, Ministry of Agriculture clearances

3. Residential and Park Development

  • Revenue Source: Real estate development, property taxes, tourism
  • Budget Allocation: ₹6,000 Crore
  • Expected Revenue: Property taxes, revenue from park admissions
  • State Permissions: Zoning and land use approvals, environmental clearances
  • Central Permissions: Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs planning approvals

4. Commercial and Small-Scale Industry Development

  • Revenue Source: Investments from multinational corporations (MNCs) and local businesses
  • Budget Allocation: ₹10,000 Crore
  • Expected Revenue: Business taxes, employment opportunities
  • State Permissions: Business licenses, land allocation
  • Central Permissions: Ministry of Commerce approvals, trade clearances
