City Design

The city development plan is divided into three phases: Phase 1 focuses on basic infrastructure like education, healthcare, and small industries with a budget of ₹25,000 crore. Phase 2 involves large-scale projects such as metro systems and business parks, with ₹175,000 crore allocated. Phase 3 targets long-term sustainability, including renewable energy and smart city infrastructure, with an investment of ₹2.2 lakh crore. The plan emphasizes growth, sustainability, and technological innovation.
