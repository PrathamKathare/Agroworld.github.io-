Phase 2: Expansion and Advanced Infrastructure Development

Budget Allocation: ₹175,000 Crore

1. Metro System

  • Revenue Source: Metro fares, government funding, and public-private partnerships
  • Budget Allocation: ₹50,000 Crore
  • Expected Revenue: Metro fares, increased property values
  • State Permissions: Urban development authority approvals, transportation permits
  • Central Permissions: Ministry of Railways and Urban Development approvals, environmental clearances

2. Airport

  • Revenue Source: Airport taxes, tourism, and business travel
  • Budget Allocation: ₹35,000 Crore
  • Expected Revenue: Airport taxes, inbound tourism, and business travel
  • State Permissions: Land allocation for airport construction, Airport Authority of India (AAI) clearance
  • Central Permissions: Ministry of Civil Aviation approvals, environmental impact assessments

3. Amusement Park and Tourism Development

  • Revenue Source: Ticket sales, hospitality services, tourism
  • Budget Allocation: ₹25,000 Crore
  • Expected Revenue: Revenue from tickets, hotels, and restaurants
  • State Permissions: Tourism department clearances, land use permissions
  • Central Permissions: Ministry of Tourism assistance, national and international promotion

4. Larger Multinational Corporations (MNCs) and Business Parks

  • Revenue Source: Business investments, exports, job creation
  • Budget Allocation: ₹40,000 Crore
  • Expected Revenue: Corporate taxes, exports-based business services
  • State Permissions: Land permissions for large businesses, industrial licensing
  • Central Permissions: Foreign investment approvals, trade and export licenses
